Brownies

Description

This brownie recipe makes rich, fudgy brownies that always turn out well. They're topped with homemade chocolate frosting to make the best brownies ever!

Ingredients

For the brownies:

  • butter
  • white sugar
  • eggs
  • vanilla extract
  • cocoa powder
  • all-purpose flour
  • salt
  • baking powder

For the frosting:

  • butter
  • cocoa powder
  • honey
  • vanilla extract
  • confectioners' sugar

Steps

  1. Make the brownies
  2. Frost the brownies
